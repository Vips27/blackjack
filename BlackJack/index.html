  • The cards are dealt to the player and to the dealer
  • One of the dealer's cards is flipped down
  • The goal of the game is to get as close to 21 without going over
  • The player has to option to get another card by clicking the "Hit Me" button
  • By clicking the "Stand" button the dealer's card is revealed and whoever is closer to 21 without going over wins
  • When calculating the total the card values are calculated in the following manner: Ace: 1 or 11 2-9: Their value 10, Jack, Queen, King: 10
  • If the first two cards are worth the same the player will be given a "Split" button Clicking that will split the player's hand into two independent hands each with one of the original cards and each hand will receive one more card
